Photo Collections

November 3, 2022 in Portfolio, Photo Collection

Intro

Time flies, and the beauty is passing quickly

Pick up a camera to capture those beautiful moments in life

And record our colorful life

「 Night like Day 」

Camera：D850
Shutter：4s
ISO：1000
Focal length：24mm
Aperture：F6.6

This photo was taken at the Tokyo Tower, I did not use a tripod because it was a tourist photo. So I pressed the camera against the sightseeing glass to try to keep it steady.

「 Candy Wheel 」

Camera：D850
Shutter：1/1600s
ISO：250
Focal length：50mm
Aperture：F3.5

This photo was taken at the subway station in Odaiba, Tokyo. Looking at this Ferris wheel from a distance, it feels like the passage of time has slowed down. I purposely edited the colors into candy colors to reflect that lively and lovely. Unfortunately, I didn’t ride the Ferris wheel at that time, and now it has been dismantled.

「 Bridge to the Sky 」

Camera：D850
Shutter：1/320s
ISO：4000
Focal length：26mm
Aperture：F2.8

This photo was taken under Tokyo Tower.
Looking up at this exquisite building with its sophisticated structure, it seems like a bridge that leads from the ground to the clouds.
The combination of power and beauty is particularly attractive in this night

「 Looking up 」

Camera：D850
Shutter：1/250s
ISO：320
Focal length：24mm
Aperture：F8

Walking between the city, shuttle in the high-rise buildings, do not forget to look up, in addition to the blue sky, there is a novelty of the landscape.

「 Inside and Outside 」

Camera：D850
Shutter：1/200s
ISO：640
Focal length：35mm
Aperture：F5.6

This photo was taken in my university’s newly built library, featuring wooden desks and see-through glass that compliments the circular architectural design.
When designing the composition, I mainly wanted to highlight the architectural features: the circular rows, reflecting a sense of extension and space. At the same time, I processed the image in post to be cooler and corrected the lens distortion.

「 Meet 」

Camera：D850
Shutter：1/320s
ISO：400
Focal length：50mm
Aperture：F3.2

This photo was taken in an izakaya. The neatly arranged wine on the shelves and the wooden clock, with the right lighting, make the whole picture present a leisurely feeling.
It is named Party, which means that a group of friends are drinking together and talking happily through the bottle of wine.

「 Collection 」

Camera：D850
Shutter：1/320s
ISO：4000
Focal length：26mm
Aperture：F2.8

The sunlight passes through the leaves and spills on the neatly arranged sake barrel walls, as if time has never flowed.
This photo was taken in Minamisando, Meiji Jingu Shrine in Tokyo.

「 Midnight Diner 」

Camera：D850
Shutter：1/400s
ISO：2000
Focal length：85mm
Aperture：F3.2

Behind the city’s bustling night scene, you can find such a small diner. It is huddled in an inconspicuous alley. With every knife and every shovel, it comforts every lonely soul.
I found this Izakaya Restaurant in the street near Asakusa Temple. It is just quietly there, waiting for everyone who comes back from physical and mental exhaustion. It makes you feel as if the meaning of the day lies there.

「 Afternoon 」

Camera：D850
Shutter：1/125s
ISO：1000
Focal length：50mm
Aperture：F2

A neat and stylized tea table reveals that beauty in a calm life. Just the right amount of spotlight highlights the visual subject well. The wooden furniture fits well with the plants. You can see how much the people who organize them love them.

「 Beesy 」

Camera：D850
Shutter：1/3200s
ISO：64
Focal length：50mm
Aperture：F3.2

In spring, all the flowers bloom and the bees begin their hard work.
When I took this photo, I didn’t focus directly on the bee, but on the flower it would choose, all I had to do was wait for it to fly and press the shutter.

「 Pure 」

Camera：D850
Shutter：1/1000s
ISO：500
Focal length：85mm
Aperture：F4

Come out of mud unsoiled
The budding lotus is very unique among the patches of foliage.

「 Busy Road 」

Camera：D850
Shutter：1/500s
ISO：400
Focal length：85mm
Aperture：F2.2

This photo was taken in Asakusa, Tokyo, Japan, at the time of the rush hour. The busy street and the passers-by waiting for the red light make a very time-sensitive work.
In post-processing, I tried to make it a special stylized, film-like effect, making the whole photo very interesting.

「 Reading Life 」

Camera：D70
Shutter：1/200s
ISO：400
Focal length：50mm
Aperture：F2

When you have nothing to do, pick up a book. The words on paper always take people to another world, as if the rush of time are slowed down
This photo was taken in an art-filled bookstore, where strangers who share a love of reading meet to sit quietly at a table and read. The colors are warm, reflecting the warm and cordial atmosphere of the setting.

「 Waiting for me 」

Camera：D3
Shutter：1/4000s
ISO：200
Focal length：70mm
Aperture：F2.8

Little boy running towards his partner with toys in hand at sunset. One can’t help but recall the sweet memories of childhood.
Telephoto lens, capture, can always capture some vivid images, and this is a good example.

「 Working at night 」

Camera：D850
Shutter：1/160s
ISO：2000
Focal length：50mm
Aperture：F1.4

By the faint light of car, worker work in the night.
All hard-working people deserve to be respected
