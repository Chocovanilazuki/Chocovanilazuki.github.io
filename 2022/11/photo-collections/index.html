Photo Collections

November 3, 2022 in Portfolio, Photo Collection

Intro

Time flies, and the beauty is passing quickly

Pick up a camera to capture those beautiful moments in life

And record our colorful life

Tokyo Night

Camera：D850
Shutter：4s
ISO：1000
Focal length：24mm
Aperture：F6.6

This photo was taken at the Tokyo Tower, I did not use a tripod because it was a tourist photo. So I pressed the camera against the sightseeing glass to try to keep it steady.

Candy Wheel

Camera：D850
Shutter：1/1600s
ISO：250
Focal length：50mm
Aperture：F3.5

This photo was taken at the subway station in Odaiba, Tokyo. Looking at this Ferris wheel from a distance, it feels like the passage of time has slowed down. I purposely edited the colors into candy colors to reflect that lively and lovely. Unfortunately, I didn’t ride the Ferris wheel at that time, and now it has been dismantled.

Busy Bee

Camera：D850
Shutter：1/3200s
ISO：64
Focal length：50mm
Aperture：F3.2

In spring, all the flowers bloom and the bees begin their hard work.
When I took this photo, I didn’t focus directly on the bee, but on the flower it would choose, all I had to do was wait for it to fly and press the shutter

New Library

Camera：D850
Shutter：1/200s
ISO：640
Focal length：35mm
Aperture：F5.6

This photo was taken in my university’s newly built library, featuring wooden desks and see-through glass that compliments the circular architectural design.
When designing the composition, I mainly wanted to highlight the architectural features: the circular rows, reflecting a sense of extension and space. At the same time, I processed the image in post to be cooler and corrected the lens distortion
