Booth Main Poster

Booth Desktop

Booth Bottom

Design software：Adobe Photoshop, Figma, Canva

This image of the MEMORRO project team’s booth backdrop creates a strong contrast between the bright red and black colors. The character illustration in the center stands out against the rotating dark background, hinting at a storyline full of mystery or conflict. The overall design makes clever use of visual layers, reflecting MEMORRO’s gothic punk style, and has a strong anime design style, attracting many young people to come and take photos!

MEMORRO · DemoDay Poster
#

Design software：Adobe Photoshop, Canva

This poster features a bright neon color palette and modern graphic design style. The title “MEMORRO” in bold capital letters dominates the bottom of the poster. Two anime-style characters, one in the foreground and one in the background, have white hair and different colored eyes. The background is a landscape with pink and blue neon lights, adding to the futuristic feel.

This poster combines modern graphic design techniques with anime art style, appealing to fans of games and animation.

MEMORRO · Marketing-Pre Poster
#

Design software：Adobe Photoshop, Canva

This 2024 marketing poster from Abstract Game Studio cleverly utilizes glitch elements, character design, and background design. The glitch art elements, the anime style character in the center, and the neon colored background work together to create an appealing futuristic theme.

MEMORRO · Boss Intro Poster
#

Design software：Adobe Photoshop, Canva

The poster uses vintage-style fonts, a choice reminiscent of the era of traditional circuses and spectacle shows. The black and white color scheme creates a sense of mystery and vintage atmosphere. This design choice also makes the text stand out. The text is arranged in a hierarchical manner to attract the eye. The size, position and thickness of the headings and subheadings have been carefully designed. Slogans and catchphrases on the posters emphasize the uniqueness and attractiveness of the exhibition.

STOP GLOBAL WARMING
#

Design software：Adobe Photoshop, Procreate

The melting popsicle is used as a metaphor for the melting glaciers, while the penguins and polar bears in the center of the picture are placed to reflect their plight and further call the audience’s attention to the climate problem of global warming.

The background is set in black to better highlight the visual subject, while the orange text is used to express the feeling of warming.

Low-carbon life
#

Design software：Adobe Photoshop, Procreate

The bottom part of this electric bulb is filled with green and brown, it just like the grass and ground and it is used as a metaphor for the earth. In order to highlight the topic: Low-carbon life, I select choose to use the Wind Power Generator as the representation. In addition, I draw a sun on the upper right, implying a bright future for such a lifestyle.

For picking colors, I really love these light colors, cuz they always make me fresh and they are very vivid. Also, it can’t miss the subject color, green, for keeping this topic in people’s minds.
